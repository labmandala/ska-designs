My Values


Authenticity & Creativity: Adding originality to inspired concepts is well worth the effort. Sharing one’s ideas with the greater community spreads inspiration and may offer improvement to an existing theory, idea, or meme. This spirit of collaboration contributes in ways we may not have anticipated.

Aesthetics & Imagination: Form and function matter. Tinker with different avenues of creative expression, seek inspiration, find beauty and healing, either in nature, online, within your tribe or within yourself and have courage to follow what draws you in. Embrace adventure and exploration.

Curiosity & Learning: Study what supports your curiosity or creative process and growth. Balance your goals, dreams, aspirations and work with healthy habits, mental and physical well being. Be adaptable and find your way through challenges. Don’t be afraid to ask for guidance when you need to.
